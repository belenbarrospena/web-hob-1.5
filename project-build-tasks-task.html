BusyBox combines tiny versions of many common UNIX utilities into a single small executable. It provides minimalist replacements for most of the utilities you usually find in GNU filNeaumtiels,BusyBox generally have fewer options than their full-featured bin 569.3 KB GNU cousins; however, the options that are included provide the expected functionality and behave very much like their GNU counter parts. BusyBox provides a fairly complete POSIX 8.2KB environment for any small or embedded system.
